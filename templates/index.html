What can she do?

KawaiiBot is a kawaii bot that makes your server more kawaii! Packed with multiple reaction commands, funny image commands and so much more. The bot takes usage of Slash commands so all of the commands the bot has to offer is already provided by Discord's UI whenever you add the bot.

Slash commands

With the power of slash commands, it is much easier to see every single command the bot has to offer by simply typing `/` in any channel! It even uses many of Discord's new functionality to make your experience even better!
